1. Identify different speakers in a conversation by analyzing the MEANING and CONTEXT of what they say
2. **CRITICALLY IMPORTANT**: Identify which speaker is the CALL CENTER OPERATOR and which is the CLIENT/CUSTOMER based on WHAT they're doing (providing service vs receiving it)

4. Format the result as JSON with role information included

**PRIMARY METHOD**: Focus on SEMANTIC CONTENT and CONVERSATION LOGIC - analyze what speakers are trying to accomplish, not just how they say it. Linguistic markers (gender, dialect) are helpful hints but NOT strict requirements. If markers conflict with meaning, trust the meaning.
